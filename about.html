Welcome to EXCEL

Excel Greentech was established in the year 2012. Our core strength lies in robust and reliable Electronics designs. Our electronics design capabilities are till the semiconductor level. We had supplied more than 5 Million LED Drivers to various brands like CG, Bajaj, Polycab, Surya, Halonix, etc.

We are people centric, quality centric and customer centric organization having more than 35 well Trained Engineers, whose total experience put together is 525 years. We are a ZERO debt company and having stable financial health.
